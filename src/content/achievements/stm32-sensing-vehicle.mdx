Designed and developed a remote-controlled environment data sensing vehicle on
STM32: embedded software, control algorithms, and real-time data processing
for monitoring environmental conditions.

Validated the platform under varied humidity, temperature, and terrain
conditions, achieving greater than 95% sensor accuracy in real-world
simulation — an early lesson in the gap between lab-clean and
field-reliable.
